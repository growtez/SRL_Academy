Frequently Asked Questions

What is SSAT?

SSAT (SRL Scholarship Admission Test) is a scholarship examination conducted by SRL Academy to identify and reward meritorious students with fee waivers on our classroom programs.

Is SSAT mandatory for all courses?

SSAT is not mandatory but highly recommended. Students can avail scholarship benefits only by appearing for and scoring well in SSAT.

What are the benefits of taking SSAT?

SSAT offers scholarships up to 100% on tuition fees, early exposure to competitive exam patterns, and helps students assess their preparation level.

Who is eligible for SSAT?

Students from Class 6 to 12 and those preparing for JEE/NEET (including droppers) are eligible to appear for SSAT.

What is the syllabus for SSAT?

The syllabus covers topics from the previous academic year. For example, for Class 11 admission, Class 10 syllabus will be tested.

What is the format of the SSAT exam?

SSAT is an objective-type (MCQ) exam with questions from Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology as applicable), and Mental Ability.

Can I take the SSAT exam more than once?

Yes, students can attempt SSAT multiple times. The best score will be considered for scholarship allocation.
