Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the SRL Scholarship Test?

SRL Scholarship Test is conducted for admission to SRL Academy Classroom Courses for JEE, NEET, and Classes 6-10. It provides students an opportunity to earn scholarships up to 100% based on their performance.

Is the scholarship test mandatory for enrollment?

No. Direct admission is also available for SRL Academy courses without appearing for the scholarship test. However, taking the test gives you a chance to avail significant fee waivers.

What is the syllabus for the scholarship test?

The syllabus is based on the curriculum of the student's current or previous class, covering core subjects relevant to their academic level - Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology.

Can I take the test more than once?

Yes. Students can appear for the scholarship test on different dates if they wish to improve their performance and increase their scholarship chances.

When will the results be announced?

Results are typically announced within 7 days of the test. Candidates will be intimated by SMS on their registered mobile number as filled in the application form.
